Important Guidelines

W9 Expirations & Winter Term

Most W9s expire in Dec/Jan and only last yearly. Ensure extra time and care at the beginning and end of terms (especially Winter) to verify a vendor is actually available and approved before planning.

Non-Standard Approvals

Certain vendors (e.g., Costco) do not accept Long-form POs, but you can still reimburse purchases using a Catering Waiver approved in advance.
